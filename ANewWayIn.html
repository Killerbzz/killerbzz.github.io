'A New Way In'
Sydney Jones Library Redesign

BA Year 2 Project

Information

Fall 2018, Individual work

Instructor: Martin Shutt

Site: Chatham St, Liverpool

Description

In this expansion project of Sydney Jones Library, the staircase network is highlighted. Through precedent studies and tests, a continuous staircase network is designed to connect each entrance in different floors. In order to guarantee the light condition and visual field from each floor, I choose glass curtain wall as facade. Moreover, to fit the Brutalism style design of the existing building, I hang pieces of brick wall decorations. This can not only form beautiful shadow effect, but also change the opacity of west facade in order to suit the requirement of the reading space.

What I hope to achieve in this project is to make the vertical circulation not only for transportation, but also a place to linger. I wish the students and staffs who use this building can reach their destination in the least time and enjoy books and communication instead.
