BA Year 2 Project

Information

Spring 2019, Individual work

Instructor: Macus Schofield

Site: Intersection of Strand St. and James St., Liverpool

Description

The project of 'the pathway _ RIBA North new headquarter' tries to form a classical mixed-use space in downtown, basing on the concept of RIBA walking tour, and the project reflects what a mixed-use building be like to approach the needs of users.

By experiencing the walking tour personally, the route shapes like a closed circle geographily. In order to make the tour more completed, I hope the building can be a part of the walking tour. Moreover, the tour shows the past glory of Liverpool architecture. While RIBA North exhibits some outstanding modern projects. Therefore, the building is a good connection of the past and the present. I hope when people visit the building, they can feel the development of the city.
